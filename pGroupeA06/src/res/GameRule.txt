The objective of the game is to be the first to cross the whole board by answering the questions asked.

The originality is on the principle of self-evaluation of his knowledge from 1 to 4 on a multitude of subjects.

Each note corresponds to a question, more or less complex.

If the answer is correct, the player will advance the number of boxes corresponding to his self-assessed score.

If the answer is incorrect, the player stays on the same square until the next turn.
